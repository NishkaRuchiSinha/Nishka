places must visit

the reader's cafe

I had visited it Feb 2020

If you are an avid reader, then you will surely love this quaint café located in Indirapuram, Ghaziabad. This place has a modern ambience featuring stylish furniture and décor has a spacious seating area. Besides a café, it also has a small library and a book store. The Reader’s Café offers an elaborate Continental menu which has everything from starters to main course dishes.
